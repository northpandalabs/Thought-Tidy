👤 Your Profile

Tell the AI who you are. When enabled, this gets prepended to every action — so instead of generic output, it writes your words, in your voice, with your context. The 👤 Sound Like Me action uses this most heavily.

Paste anything — a bio, a company description, writing samples, a list of your common phrases.

💡 Advanced: This is the poor-man's MCP — maintain your personal context as a plain text file anywhere on the web, and load it here. For a true Model Context Protocol setup (connecting AI to live databases, files, APIs), pair this extension with Claude Desktop which has native MCP support.
