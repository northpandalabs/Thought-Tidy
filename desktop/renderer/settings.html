Context & Audience

When you run an action, tap + Add context in the popup to tell the AI who you're writing for. Built-in levels cover Beginner to Expert. Pro users can add custom audience types for specific domains or professions — like Firefighter or iOS Developer — so the AI uses the right vocabulary.
